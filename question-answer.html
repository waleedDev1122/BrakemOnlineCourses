Sanitary Equipment

When exposed to chemicals, the skin determines the risks of which substance it is exposed to, how long it is exposed to the substance and skin condition. Personal hygiene iS important. If we care the skin, the natural protection of the skin can be kept intact. Thermosetting plastic components that come into contact with the skin should be washed away as soon as possible. If the components are left and cure on the skin, it becomes much harder to wash them away and special cleansers must de used to clean the skin. Therefore, it is important for proximity to laundry facilities. Washing facilities with hot and cold water should be in connection with the rest area. Shower must be in connection with changing room. In the immediate vicinity of the workplace, it should in most cases be possible for eye wash and emergency shower. What is required specifically should have been identified in the risk assessment. It should be possible to find eye shower or emergency shower even when blinded by chemist in the eyes. They should also be available in a short period of time.

Signage

Signature indicating that thermosetting work is in progress should be set up in connection with the workplace and on the doors to the premises. These signs are to be posted on the door of the premises or area to inform employed persons other than those handling the chemical products. If the risk assessment has indicated that the work does not entail any risk of exposure to other employees, the sign can be omitted.
